Steps to activate a new plan:
1. Ask the customer for their:
    - full name per government-issued ID
    - email
    - last 4 digits and letter if their NRIC e.g. 123A
2. Ask the customer if they want a pre-paid or post-paid plan
3. Ask the customer what requires they have for the plans
4. Refer to plan resources and recommend the best plan for the customer
5. Ask the customer if they want to port their existing number
6. If they want to port their existing number, ask them for their existing number and confirm it with them
7. Use program purchase_plan to create a new plan
