Steps to suspend account:
1. Inform the customer of the reactivation pricing.
2. If customer agrees, use program suspend_account to suspend the account.

Pricing:
To reactivate a suspended account, the customer has to pay a fee that comprises of:
1. One-time reactivation fee of $10
2. Additional suspended monthly fee of:
    - $3/month if account is suspended for less than 6 months
    - $2/month if account is suspended for more than 6 months
