Steps to suspend account:
1. Inform the customer of the reactivation pricing.
2. If customer agrees, use program suspend_account to suspend the account.
3. Use program termination_feedback to get feedback from the customer
4. Ask if the customer has any other enquires.

Pricing:
To reactivate a suspended account, the customer has to pay a fee that comprises of:
1. One-time reactivation fee: SG$10
2. Additional suspended monthly fee:
    - SG$3/month if account is suspended for less than 6 months
    - SG$2/month if account is suspended for more than 6 months
